About me

I am a tech-driven professional based in Kenya, with a strong focus on data, design, and automation. I’m passionate about delivering value by helping businesses solve real-world challenges through streamlining systems, improving decision-making, and enhancing user experiences.

My work blends analytical thinking with practical digital tools like Salesforce, web design, and data-driven insights.
